OkiStrue Support

Welcome to the official OkiStrue support page.

OkiStrue is a space dedicated to the world of dogs, where you can share videos of alerts, dogs looking for adoption, fun moments, and special memories of our four-legged friends.

If you need technical assistance, want to report an issue, or wish to contact us for general information, you can write to the addresses below.

Support email: support@okistrue.com

Reports email: support@okistrue.com

General contacts email: support@okistrue.com

For faster support

In your message, we suggest including:

  • the type of issue you are experiencing;
  • your device model;
  • the app version, if available;
  • a brief description of the problem or request.
